How to play?



When game started, you see a question like "DOWN OR RIGHT". You should click the arrows.
Examples answers:
UP:↑
REVERSE OF RIGHT:←
HORIZONTAL:→ or ←
VERTICAL:↑ or ↓

If you click buy, you gain an arrow and your multipler increase.

If you click an arrow, arrow on game change arrow that you click.
